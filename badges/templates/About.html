About EcoChallenge

EcoChallenge is a web application designed to encourage and track environmentally friendly habits. Our goal is to motivate users to make positive changes in their daily lives through a series of engaging challenges.

How It Works

Users can browse a list of available challenges, each with a specific goal and point value. Once a user accepts a challenge, they can submit proof of completion to earn points and climb the leaderboard.

  • Browse Challenges: Discover a variety of eco-friendly tasks.
  • Accept Challenges: Choose the challenges that you want to participate in.
  • Submit Proof: Upload evidence of your completed tasks for review.
  • Earn Points: Gain points for every approved submission.
  • Climb the Leaderboard: Compete with other users to become an Eco-Champion.

Our Mission

We believe that small individual actions can collectively make a big impact on the environment. EcoChallenge provides a fun and interactive platform to inspire sustainable living and foster a community of eco-conscious individuals.

The Team

This application was created by a passionate team of developers dedicated to making a positive impact on the planet.
